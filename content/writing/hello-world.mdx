# Starting Here

I'm Samuel — a full-stack engineer who spends most of his time building at the intersection of AI, Web3, and product.

This is where I write. Not a newsletter, not a content calendar — just a place to think out loud about the things I care about.

That usually means:

- **AI and agentic tools** — how they're changing the way we build, what's hype, what's actually useful
- **Web3 and blockchain** — onchain systems, DeFi, smart contracts, and where it's all actually going
- **Frontend and product design** — the craft of building interfaces that feel good, not just function
- **Code and architecture** — decisions I've made, tradeoffs I've had to think through, patterns that hold up
- **Products** — the gap between shipping a feature and shipping something people actually want

I build things and I think about why things are built the way they are. Writing is how I make sense of both.

If any of it is useful to you, great. If not, that's fine too.
